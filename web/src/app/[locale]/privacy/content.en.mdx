# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2025-07-01**

## 1. Introduction

Context Space is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our context engineering infrastructure platform.

By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, username, and password
- **Profile Information**: Additional profile details you choose to provide
- **Configuration Data**: Integration settings, preferences, and custom configurations
- **Support Communications**: Information you provide when contacting customer support

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our platform, features used, and frequency of use
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
- **Log Data**: Server logs, error reports, and system performance metrics
- **Analytics Data**: User behavior patterns and platform performance metrics

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Integrations
When you connect third-party services to Context Space, we may collect:
- **Authentication Tokens**: OAuth tokens and API keys for service access
- **Service Data**: Data you authorize us to access from connected services (GitHub repositories, Slack messages, Airtable records, etc.)
- **Integration Metadata**: Information about your connected services and their configurations
- **Synchronized Content**: Data that flows between your connected services through our platform

### 2.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies
- **Essential Cookies**: Required for platform functionality and security
- **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how our platform is used
- **Preference Cookies**: Remember your settings and preferences
- **Third-Party Cookies**: From integrated services and analytics providers

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- **Encryption**: Data encryption in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access and authentication systems
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
- **Vault Security**: Secure credential storage using HashiCorp Vault

### 5.2 Third-Party Security
- OAuth tokens and API keys are stored securely and encrypted
- We follow security best practices for all third-party integrations
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

### 5.3 Data Breach Response
In the event of a data breach:
- We will investigate and contain the incident promptly
- Affected users will be notified as required by law
- We will cooperate with relevant authorities as necessary
